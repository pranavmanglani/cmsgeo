Week 1: Socio-Economic Insights

In our research across Italy, Nepal, Gujarat, and Turkey, we discovered that vulnerability is socio-economic. The magnitude of an earthquake is constant, but the impact depends on the wealth of the nation and the enforcement of building codes.
