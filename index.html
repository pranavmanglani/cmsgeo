Essential Safety Precautions

1. DROP, COVER, HOLD ON

The primary indoor safety protocol. Protect your head and neck under sturdy furniture.

2. AVOID HAZARDS

Stay away from windows, glass, and tall furniture. Use stairs, never elevators.

3. EMERGENCY KIT

Maintain 72 hours of water, food, and first-aid in an easy-to-grab bag.
