GEOGRAPHY
EARTHQUAKE
Earthquakes occur due to sudden energy release in the Earth's crust caused by tectonic stress.
The lithosphere is broken into tectonic plates that constantly move and interact.
Stress builds at plate boundaries until rocks fracture and seismic waves form.
The focus is the origin point underground, while the epicenter lies above it.
Understanding earthquakes helps design structures that resist seismic damage.
