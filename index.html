Interactive Seismic Model

In our project, we focused on three key components of an earthquake:

  • The Focus (Hypocenter): The point inside the Earth where the rupture starts.
  • The Epicenter: The point directly above the focus on the surface, where shaking is often strongest.
  • Structural Failure: Note how taller buildings or those with poor foundations (represented by the "crumbling" effect) react differently to the release of energy.
