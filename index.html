Topic: EARTHQUAKE

EARTHQUAKE
THEORY
Earthquakes are caused by sudden release of energy in the Earth's crust due to tectonic stress.
The lithosphere is divided into tectonic plates that move slowly over the asthenosphere.
Stress builds at plate boundaries until rocks fracture along faults.
Energy travels as seismic waves that cause ground shaking.
Understanding earthquakes helps design earthquake-resistant structures.
